Seiun Sky is a standout character in the Umamusume: Pretty Derby franchise, known for her laid-back "slacker" persona that masks a brilliant tactical mind.

Biography

Does what she wants, when she wants. As free as a drifting cloud...

As one of the most easygoing girls you'll meet, she lives life at her own pace. But on the inside, she's quite the schemer, and her laziness is just an act to get others to let down their guard... sometimes. Her hobbies include taking afternoon naps and fishing. She also loves cats and is often seen curling up with them outside on the grass. She's classmates with Special Week.

Appearance

Seiun Sky embodies an easygoing lifestyle, reflected in her relaxed appearance. Blue eyes with short, fluffy, faded green hair. Her right ear is adorned with a green cover featuring soft cloud patterns and fluffy white puffs (most likely to resemble clouds) at the base. A small sunflower patch adorns the side of her head, and a brown choker featuring a horseshoe design.

Her outfit complements her laid-back demeanor with a fashionably simple yet charming basic white blouse accented with subtle hints of green. The blouse features somewhat "wild" ruffles at the chest, sleeves, and waist, with the waistline flaring out in a skirt-like fashion. Paired with this are green shorts adorned with cute cloud-like patterns that echo the same design as her ear. A set of straps and a yellow-and-green band wrap her left thigh. And finishing her look are basic gray heels, embellished with fluffy cloud-like puffs that, like her shorts, mirror the ones on her ear.
